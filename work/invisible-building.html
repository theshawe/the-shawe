As mentioned on my home-page I'm self employed and trade as Invisible Building. I design, code, strategise, build and grow brands. With continuous study and natural intuition, I specialise in design for the web and my core source of income is through selling web to local business, creatives and fellow entrepreneurs.

In 2016 I started working for my largest client, a Northern Irish startup steeped in history, after six months contracting with Chain Reaction Cycles I was offered a full time permanent contract working with the company as their Email Development Executive. I took this job and continued to work there until April 2019.

Between 2016 and 2019 I continued to trade as Invisible Building. However I put the business on a three year Hiatus while I focused my energy on Email Development, Personalisation and Automation for CRC.
